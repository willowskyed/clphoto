My Story

A little background on me: I was born and raised in a small mountain town called Revelstoke, which invoked in me a love of the outdoors. My interest in photography began when I was given my first camera at 7 years old, and grew as I started my photography page in high-school. Upon completing business school I’d learned what it takes to make my hobby into a career, and I took a leap of faith; giving up the security of a full-time job with benefits to spend a year pursuing my dream and seeing where it would take me. For the past 2 years I’ve poured all of my time, energy and money into my business. I’m now booking weddings into 2024! It’s been an exciting process watching my skills grow and my style evolve, meeting so many amazing people and being able to capture their most special moments. My current goals are to expand more in the wedding industry, help other businesses with marketing and branding, open a studio for winter and an online shop for landscape photography, and to try out wildlife photography!
What I’ll bring to our shoots: the Sony A7 II with a few different lenses depending on the type of shoot, my current favourite is a 50mm F1.8 fixed lens. I’ll also bring my creativity, adventurous-side, and outgoing energy that will make you feel comfortable in front of the camera.

My passion is to capture candid moments that radiate love.
