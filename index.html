My Story

My name is Cassidy. I am a fun-loving photographer who strives to get the most beautiful, natural photos of you in your element. My passion is to capture moments and memories you can hold onto forever.
When it comes to me as a photographer, you will find I am very outgoing and passionate about my work. I aim to make you as comfortable as possible and for you to enjoy your time getting pictures taken! I have a very adventurous and creative mind that allows me to help brainstorm ideas with you for your photoshoot! I love to hear your ideas too; this helps me picture what you are looking for.
